About GridGenius

GridGenius is a fine-tuned LLM assistant designed for optimizing national energy generation and usage. Trained on datasets including real-time consumption, climate, and regional patterns, it empowers operators, researchers, and policy makers with actionable forecasts and insights.

  • Created by Adib and team
  • Combines time-series models with transformers
  • Focused on explainability + sustainability
